# Store-Sales-Dashboard
## **1.Project Title**
Super Store Sales Dashboard – Power BI Analytics

## **2.Short Description / Purpose**
The **Super Store Sales Dashboard** is an interactive business intelligence report developed using **Power BI** to analyze retail sales performance. The dashboard provides insights into key metrics such as **total sales, profit, quantity sold, and average sales performance** across different regions, categories, segments, and shipping modes.
The purpose of this dashboard is to help business stakeholders quickly understand sales trends, identify high-performing product categories, monitor regional performance, and make data-driven decisions to improve profitability and operational efficiency.

## **3.Tech Stack**
The following technologies and tools were used to build the dashboard:
* **Microsoft Power BI** – Data visualization and dashboard development
* **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Used to create calculated measures and KPIs
* **Power Query** – Used for data transformation and cleaning
* **Data Modeling** – For establishing relationships between data fields.

  
## **4.Data Source**
The dataset used for this project is the **Super Store Sales dataset**, which contains retail transactional data including:
* Order details
* Customer information
* Product categories and subcategories
* Sales and profit values
* Shipping modes
* Payment methods
* Geographic sales data (state, region, city)

The dataset was imported into **Microsoft Power BI** from **Google Drive**, then transformed using **Power Query** for analysis.

## **5.Features and Highlights**

### **1. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)**

The dashboard displays important metrics at the top for quick insights:

* **Total Sales:** 341K
* **Total Quantity Sold:** 5239
* **Total Profit:** 27K
* **Average Sales Value:** 4

These KPIs help decision-makers quickly evaluate overall business performance.
### **2. Sales by Category**
A horizontal bar chart shows sales distribution across major product categories:
* Office Supplies
* Furniture
* Technology
This helps identify the highest revenue-generating product category.

### **3. Sales Trend Analysis (Year-over-Year / Monthly)**
Line charts visualize how sales change over time, allowing users to:
* Identify seasonal trends
* Detect growth or decline periods
* Compare monthly performance
* 
### **4. Sales by Segment**
A donut chart shows customer segments such as:
* Consumer
* Corporate
* Home Office
This helps businesses understand which segment contributes most to revenue.

### **5. Sales by Shipping Mode**
Displays sales distribution by shipping methods such as:
* Standard Class
* Second Class
* First Class
* Same Day
This helps evaluate logistics and shipping preferences.

### **6. Sales and Profit by State (Map Visualization)**
A geographic map visualizes sales and profit distribution across different states in the United States, helping identify:
* High-performing regions
* Underperforming areas
* Regional business opportunities

### **7. Payment Mode Analysis**
A donut chart shows the distribution of payment methods such as:
* Cash on Delivery
* Online Payments
* Card Payments
This insight helps businesses understand customer payment preferences.

### **8. Sub-Category Performance**
A bar chart highlights top-performing product subcategories like:
* Chairs
* Binders
* Phones

This helps identify the most profitable products.
✅ **Overall Benefit:**
The dashboard enables stakeholders to **monitor performance, identify trends, and make strategic business decisions using real-time visual insights.**
